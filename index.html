About BRADZA

A group of four brothers united by vision, purpose, and the drive to build generational wealth.

Our Origin

Founded by four Kenyans living in Cairns, Australia, BRADZA Co Ltd represents the fusion of Kenyan entrepreneurial spirit with Australian opportunity.

Our Mission

To accumulate resources through weekly savings and strategic lending, creating a portfolio that enables substantial joint investments.

Our Model

Weekly contributions form the foundation. We offer loans to members at fair interest rates, generating returns that fuel our collective growth.

IB&B Brand

Investmates Brothers and Beyond - our social identity connecting us with a global community of builders and wealth creators.
